At Staydate, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and sharing of personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: such as your name, email address, and phone number when you fill out forms, register, or contact us.
  • Usage Data: including details about your interactions with our website, such as pages visited, time spent, and other analytics.
  • Cookies and Tracking Data: we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze activity and improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website and services.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website.
  • Communicate with you, including responding to your requests and updates about our services.
  • Monitor usage and analyze trends to better understand how users interact with our site.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share data with trusted service providers to help us operate and improve our website. All third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

4. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data and employ appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

You may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including access, correction, deletion, and objection to processing. To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@staydate.africa.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. This Privacy Policy applies only to our website, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices of other websites.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@staydate.africa.
